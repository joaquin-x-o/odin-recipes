Tex-Mex Burger

Hey there, burger enthusiasts! Welcome to our Tex-Mex Burger delight, where bold flavors and hearty satisfaction collide. Get ready to embark on a taste adventure that celebrates the vibrant spirit of Tex-Mex cuisine.

Prepare your taste buds for a culinary adventure with our Tex-Mex Burger. Imagine sinking your teeth into a perfectly grilled burger patty, infused with a tantalizing blend of spices that provide just the right amount of heat. Picture the richness of melted cheese enveloping the patty, complemented by the smooth creaminess of the Cajun mayo, adding a zesty and fiery kick that elevates every bite.

Each layer, from the seasoned patty to the Cajun mayo, is carefully crafted to deliver a burst of flavors with every mouthful. Whether you're a spice enthusiast or someone looking to add a little excitement to your mealtime, this burger is sure to hit the spot

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 ⅓ pounds ground beef sirloin
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
  • ½ cup diced white onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 slices pepperjack cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • 4 leaves lettuce
  • 4 slices tomato

Directions

  1. Preheat grill for medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together the mayonnaise and 1 teaspoon of Cajun seasoning. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground sirloin, jalapeno pepper, onion, garlic, 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning, and Worcestershire sauce using your hands. Divide into 4 balls, and flatten into patties.
  3. Lightly oil the grilling surface, and place the patties on the grill. Cook for about 5 minutes per side, or until well done. During the last 2 minutes, lay a slice of cheese on top of each patty. Spread the seasoned mayonnaise onto the insides of the buns. Put burgers in the buns, and top with lettuce and tomato to serve.
